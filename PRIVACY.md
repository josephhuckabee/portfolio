# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Built by Joseph handles information submitted through `builtbyjoseph.com`.

## Information Collected

The site may collect information you choose to submit through the contact form, including:

- Name
- Email address
- Message content

The contact form also includes a hidden anti-spam field. If that field is filled, the submission may be treated as spam.

## How Information Is Used

Submitted information is used to:

- Respond to project inquiries
- Understand the request you sent
- Prevent spam or misuse of the contact form

The site does not sell personal information.

## Third-Party Services

Contact form submissions may be processed by an email delivery provider, currently Resend, so the message can be delivered to Joseph Huckabee.

If the site is deployed through a hosting provider, that provider may also collect standard server logs such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and request time.

## Cookies and Tracking

This site does not intentionally use advertising cookies or behavioral tracking.

The site may store a theme preference in your browser using `localStorage` so light or dark mode can persist between visits.

## Data Retention

Contact messages may be retained in email records as long as reasonably needed to respond, manage project history, or comply with ordinary business recordkeeping.

## Your Choices

You can request that a previous contact message be deleted from active records by sending a request through the contact page or by email, if you have Joseph's direct email address.

## Security

Reasonable steps are used to keep the site simple and reduce risk, but no website or email system can guarantee absolute security.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the site or contact process changes. The effective date above should be updated when meaningful changes are made.
