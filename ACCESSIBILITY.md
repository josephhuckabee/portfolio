# Accessibility Statement

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Built by Joseph aims to provide a site that is usable by as many people as possible.

## Current Accessibility Practices

The site includes:

- Semantic HTML landmarks and headings
- Keyboard-accessible navigation and controls
- Visible focus styles
- Skip link to main content
- Text labels for form fields
- Descriptive alternative text for meaningful images
- Responsive layouts for small screens
- Reduced-motion support where motion is used

## Known Limits

This is a small portfolio site maintained by one person. Some third-party links, external project previews, or embedded browser behavior may be outside this site's direct control.

## Feedback

If you find an accessibility issue, please use the contact page and include:

- The page or feature where the issue happened
- What you expected to happen
- Your browser, device, or assistive technology if relevant

Accessibility feedback is welcome and will be treated as a real bug report.
